Good Friend • Birthday reminders that never fail

Swetrasoft builds great software for human relationships.

Our flagship product Good Friend is a state of the art birthday reminder app designed for everyone that never wants to miss a birthday again. You can explore Western and Chinese horoscopes and find out which famous persons were born on the same day as your contact. Sync your LinkedIn contacts for easy access to their birthdays and get LLM created customer tailored suggestions for birthday messages that draw on all information that is known about your contacts.

Privacy-first: Your contacts stay yours. Multi-platform: Web, iOS, and Android.

About Swetrasoft
